About Dent-in

One connected system, from booking to diagnosis.

Dent-in unifies the patient-facing app with a full clinical backend: patients, dentists, appointments, prescriptions, and AI-assisted teeth scans — one product, end to end.

For patients

Register, find a verified dentist, book in seconds, track your live queue, upload photos for an AI dental check, and get prescriptions straight to your dashboard.

For clinics

A Spring Boot REST API manages patients, dentists, appointments with slot-conflict protection, prescriptions, and teeth-scan analysis records in one place.

AI teeth analysis

Upload front, left and right photos and the scanner returns a health score, detected issues, and follow-up recommendations — the same pipeline the clinic backend exposes.
